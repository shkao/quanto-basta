area module¶

area.calculate_area(radius)¶

Calculates the area of a circle given its radius.

The calling syntax is: area = calculate_area(radius)

The calculated area is given by the formula π * radius^2.

Parameters:

radius – The radius of the circle

Returns:

The area of the circle
